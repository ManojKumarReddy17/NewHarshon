Jewellery Software

  • Harshon Technologies is a leading name in jewellery software development, delivering innovative and reliable solutions. Our software is designed to streamline your business operations, offering features for sales, purchases, payments, and more.
  • Accounting Reports: Day book, accounts, profit/loss, interest calculation, cash/bank book.
  • Purchase Reports: Raw materials, finished products, order generation, gold purchases.
  • Sales Reports: Customer orders, delivery, sales returns, VAT calculation, order cancellations.
  • Money Lending: Cash received, interest calculations, loss reports.
  • Aging Analysis: Debtors/creditors balance, bill-by-bill analysis.
  • Inventory Control: Stock of raw materials, old gold, soldering, and unfinished products.
  • Backup & Restore: Secure database backup and restore functionality.
  • Multi-user Accessibility: Access and collaborate seamlessly with multiple users.
  • Cost-effective Solution: Affordable software solution designed for scalability.
  • Customization: Tailor the software to meet the unique needs of your jewellery business.
  • Tracking from Creation to Sale: Full tracking of your jewellery from creation to final sale.
